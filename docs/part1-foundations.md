# Part I: Foundations of SHL Assessment

This section establishes the theoretical and historical context for SHL assessments, introducing the core instruments and their workplace applications.

## Learning Goals

By completing Part I, you will:
- Understand the three-pillar architecture of SHL assessment (OPQ32, Verify, MQ)
- Recognize how personality, ability, and motivation combine for talent prediction
- Appreciate the role of the Universal Competency Framework (UCF) as the unifying architecture
- Distinguish between Classical Test Theory and Item Response Theory approaches
