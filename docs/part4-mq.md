# Part IV: The Motivation Questionnaire (MQ)

Measurement of workplace motivators and their role in talent development, engagement, and retention.

## Chapters in This Part

| Chapter | Title | Description |
|---------|-------|-------------|
| 15 | MQ Theoretical Foundations | McClelland, Herzberg, Self-Determination Theory |
| 16 | The 18 Motivation Dimensions | Energy, Synergy, Intrinsic, Extrinsic domains |
| 17 | MQ Construction and Scoring | Likert format, CTT scoring, Cronbach's alpha |
| 18 | MQ in Practice | Coaching, HiPo identification, Stay/Rise vectors |

## Learning Goals

By completing Part IV, you will:
- Understand all 18 motivation dimensions and their workplace implications
- Distinguish MQ's CTT scoring approach from OPQ32's IRT methodology
- Apply MQ for development, coaching, and retention planning
- Identify High Potential candidates using the Ability/Aspiration/Engagement model
