# Chapter 29: Industry Quality Convergence

{: .note }
> **Note:** This chapter is currently under development. Content for Part VII (Comparative Analysis) will be added in a future update.

## Learning Objectives

- Understand the convergence of quality standards across assessment providers
- Analyze how methodological advances have raised industry baselines
- Evaluate the competitive landscape of psychometric assessment
- Compare technical standards across major assessment publishers

## Key Takeaways

- The psychometric assessment industry has experienced significant convergence in quality standards
- IRT-based methodologies have become industry standard for cognitive assessment
- Forced-choice personality measures are increasingly adopted across providers
- Regulatory and professional guidelines drive minimum quality thresholds
- Competition focuses on innovation rather than basic psychometric quality
