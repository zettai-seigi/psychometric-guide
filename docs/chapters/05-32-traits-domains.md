## The Three Major Domains

The OPQ32 organizes 32 distinct personality traits into three major domains:

### 1. Relationships with People
This domain captures interpersonal style, including:
- How individuals interact with others
- Preferences for influence and leadership
- Collaboration and support behaviors
- Social confidence and interpersonal approaches

### 2. Thinking Style
This domain measures cognitive preferences, including:
- Approach to information and analysis
- Creativity and innovation preferences
- Organizational and structural preferences
- Detail orientation and conscientiousness

### 3. Feelings and Emotions
This domain assesses emotional style, including:
- Emotional resilience and stress management
- Drive, energy, and competitive orientation
- Confidence and optimism
- Coping strategies and emotional control
